# 17 Quotes to Motivate You to Want to Read ...

Reading is healthy for you, because it'll keep your mind active and your imagination vivid. If you haven't picked up a novel in a while, it's time to change that. Here are a few quotes that'll motivate you to read:

## 1. Journey of a Lifetime

![Journey of a Lifetime](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dn/mj/ilifu7qp570e40264f4de922035529_600x849.jpg)

You don't have to spend money on a plane ticket in order to go on an adventure. There are tiny adventures contained inside of every book that you see.

## 2. Someplace to Go

![Someplace to Go](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/z2/b6s9iynl570e40261a1ac687903242_403x503.jpg)

If you hate where you are, but aren't able to leave, a book is a great way to escape. It'll take you away from your problems, even if it's only for an hour or two.

## 3. Magic Happens

![Magic Happens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ts/nq/wvnmasm2570e402637b72358956789_570x712.jpg)

J.K. Rowling knows all about magic.

## 4. A Thousand Lives

![A Thousand Lives](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i8/wf/jroay2kf570e40270951e414723673_500x667.jpg)

You're always going to be you. That's never going to change. The closest you'll get to living another life is by opening up a book and reading a story about somebody else.

## 5. The More You Read, the More You Know

![The More You Read, the More You Know](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cf/hh/ts8var4y570e402b6011d466172928_600x576.jpg)

Reading makes you smarter, and we all know that the smartest people become the most successful people.

## 6. Worse than Burning Books

![Worse than Burning Books](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/k9/p6cge84z570e4026d8ee2341836831_500x750.jpg)

What's the point of books if you don't read them? Give them a purpose by flipping them open. They deserve to be read.

## 7. One Life

![One Life](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ug/j1/j4vpfviz570e402bc7522032472997_540x763.jpg)

Don't settle for one boring life. Live thousands of exciting ones, instead.

## 8. Reading is Exercise for the Mind

![Reading is Exercise for the Mind](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dr/p3/u612fsfx570e4027e65bb330264532_465x528.jpg)

You hop on the treadmill to keep your body in shape. So why aren't you opening up books to keep your mind in shape?

## 9. The Man WHo Does Not Read

![The Man WHo Does Not Read](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o5/m7/mu42tp5h570e4028051d7108491269_600x600.jpg)

If you don't read, you might as well be illiterate, because you're not putting your skill to use.

## 10. Reading is like Breathing

![Reading is like Breathing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d6/jh/d1itephw570e40283cd34318148804_600x685.jpg)

You need books as much as you need air. They're both crucial to living a healthy life.

## 11. Reading Isn't Boring

![Reading Isn't Boring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/vu/wyx64umj570e40ab9b7ac471608554_460x460.jpg)

If you hate reading, you've been picking up the wrong books. Try looking for something that interests you instead of reading the trending novels.

## 12. People She Could Never Be

![People She Could Never Be](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kk/xt/nmp9latn570e40293377c310992876_480x720.jpg)

This sounds sad, but it's actually thrilling. Books can give you the things you might never get in reality.

## 13. Between the Pages of a Book

![Between the Pages of a Book](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b6/y1/gtp6o3ql570e4029229f6101808213_460x644.jpg)

There's no place more comforting.

## 14. Two Places at the Same Time

![Two Places at the Same Time](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/ai/ouc5rbjy570e402b90970573583249_600x817.jpg)

Do you wish you could do magic? Well, if you read a book, you'll be performing a special trick. You'll be in two different places at the same time.

## 15. A Good Book

![A Good Book](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zw/fq/h9rl6yv6570e4029ce0d2421311769_600x800.jpg)

If you have something to look forward to all day, then your day won't feel as stressful. that's why you should always have a book on your nightstand.

## 16. Escape, Comfort, and Consolation

![Escape, Comfort, and Consolation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/az/dm/lhramllg570e4029e3433117361359_480x480.jpg)

Reading can help you in more ways than one. It can help comfort you when you're lonely, escape when you're upset, and stimulate you when you want to think.

## 17. Never Judge a Book

![Never Judge a Book](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pp/je/eefwwued570e402f3c69a257722373_600x450.jpg)

Everyone knows that most movies suck in comparison to their books. So give that novel a chance. It's probably going to impress you more than whatever you saw up on the big screen.

Books can keep your mind healthy, which is why you should never stop reading! What book would you recommend to everyone else out there?
