# 7 Awesome Free Books to Download to Your Kindle ...

The best free books to download are the classics, because they’re public domain. Even though the stories are old, they’re still relatable. All of these novels are fantastic pieces of literature that deserve all of the praise they’ve received over the years. If you’re looking for some free books to download that’ll entertain and delight, start right here:

## 1. The Time Machine by H.G. Wells

![The Time Machine by H.G. Wells](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t7/mx/nz/seofzea6_520x849.jpg)

This novel will fly by, so don’t worry if you don’t have much time to read. It tells the story of a time traveler who lands in the year 802,701. He meets the two different types of humans that have survived throughout the millennia, and tries to find his stolen time machine so he can return to his own time. Wells was the first person to coin the word ‘time machine,’ which we use in science fiction stories to this day. This is one of the classic, free books to download that provides commentary on our government and society as a whole.

## 2. The Wonderful Wizard of Oz by L. Frank Baum

![The Wonderful Wizard of Oz by L. Frank Baum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sw/ca/qr/pe6luwnj_600x809.jpg)

You’ve all seen the film, but have you read the book? Although it’s much different to the movie, it still involves a girl named Dorothy who ends up in the Land of Oz after a tornado. There are actually fourteen different Oz books, and the movie only covers the events in the first book. There’s so much more to learn about Oz, and you never even knew it!

## 3. Little Women by Louisa May Alcott

![Little Women by Louisa May Alcott](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p5/pa/fy/juh1lzla_600x925.jpg)

If you like stories about women and gender roles, then this is the novel for you. It takes place in America after the Civil War. Four sisters learn about the world as they grow up together. If you want to find out more, try downloading it.

## 4. Moby Dick by Herman Melville

![Moby Dick by Herman Melville](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wo/j7/ib/qn8l017d_600x911.jpg)

This is a novel you should only read if you have time and patience. There are many long descriptions that can turn a reader off. Of course, it is considered a classic, which means it’s worth it in the end. If you want to challenge yourself and think you can handle a bit of tough reading, why not download it?

## 5. Frankenstein by Mary Shelley

![Frankenstein by Mary Shelley](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kl/j3/vy/05blidfi_599x797.jpg)

If you haven’t read Frankenstein yet, you should do so. Shelley wrote it after a group of her equals decided to have a competition to see who could create the best horror story. Clearly, she did well. This story is compelling, and asks a big question about what is considered human.

## 6. The Scarlet Letter by Nathaniel Hawthorne

![The Scarlet Letter by Nathaniel Hawthorne](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/j0/pp/getkqola_600x915.jpg)

The film Easy A, starring Emma Stone, was partially inspired by this novel. It’s about a woman who is unfaithful to her husband, and is sentenced to wear a scarlet A on her clothing in order to shame her. It shows how cruel people can be to women who do not adhere to society’s rules. Although it was written in the 1800s, it contains ideas that are still contemplated to this day.

## 7. The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn by Mark Twain

![The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn by Mark Twain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/tx/mq/dzxyri68_600x1005.jpg)

This story takes place in the south, and was published in 1884. Since it was written so long ago, it includes stereotypical ideas that nowadays can be found offensive. If you’re able to push past the ignorance of the time, then the book is fascinating. It’s definitely a story you should plan on reading.

Even though they’re old, these novels are worth your time. Are you a fan of any of these stories?
