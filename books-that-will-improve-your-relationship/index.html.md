# 3 Books That Will Improve Your Relationship ...

There are loads of **books that will improve your relationship**. It comes as no surprise to me, but did you know that one of the most popular and thriving genres of literature, one that has been going strong now for a long time, is that of the self-help book/advice book? Fifty years ago, a woman might have turned to her mother for some good old fashioned romantic advice, but these days it is much more likely that she would head to the nearest bookshop to pick up a book that provides an ‘expert’ opinion. One of the most popular topics is love, dating and all that goes with trying to navigate the waters of relationships. The thing is, there are so many books that will improve your relationship that it’s hard to know which are the best to settle on. I can help. If you have been having a few problems with your partner recently, then check out these three **books that will improve your relationship**.

## 1. The 5 Love Languages by Gary Chapman

![text, purple, violet, advertising, font,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lr/so/p3hm687k5b19114dcbad2601756356_500x500.jpg)

It’s an important lesson to learn that not everybody loves in the same sorts of ways, and this book can help you to understand that just because you and your partner express your feelings in different ways, it doesn’t make one method better than the other. You might show yourself through physical touch, whereas he might show it through words of affirmation. These types of insights can be really important and revelatory when trying to work out what is going on between you. That's why this is one of the best **books that will improve your relationship**.

Buy at [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Men Are from Mars, Women Are from Venus by John Gray

![text, font, line, area, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bk/k9/hxumva1e5b19114e98288207924165_500x500.jpg)

This is an oldie but still a goodie! This classic self-help romance book gives you expert insights into the different ways that both men and woman operate, and in turn the ways that these actions and choices can be interpreted by the opposite sex. Your relationship might not be on the rocks, it might simply be suffering from a lack of understanding and communication. This tried and tested book will be able to help you with this.

Buy at [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Don’t Sweat the Small Stuff in Love by Richard & Kristine Carlson

![text, pink, purple, font, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ym/gt/ipbtynp85b19114e75607472330552_423x500.jpg)

This is a book that will help you to appreciate your partner in ways that you might not have considered before. It will teach you to let go of old anger and annoyances and free up some space for happiness and sweetness to work its way in instead.

Buy at [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
