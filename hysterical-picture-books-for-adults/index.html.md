# 7 Hysterical Picture Books for Adults ...

Children aren't the only ones who get to read picture books. There are a few of them meant for an adult's eyes. According to [Bustle](https://www.bustle.com/articles/131632-11-picture-books-that-are-meant-for-adults), here are some hysterical picture books filled with adult content:

## 1. If You Give a Kid a Cookie, Will He Shut the F up?: a Parody for Adults by Marcy Roznick

![If You Give a Kid a Cookie, Will He Shut the F up?: a Parody for Adults by Marcy Roznick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q3/bp/xugjoz0z5697d324686cc590304684_600x696.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

You know all about giving a mouse a cookie, and now it's time to read about what happens when you give a kid a cookie.

## 2. Do You Want to Play with My Balls? by the Cifaldi Brothers

![Do You Want to Play with My Balls? by the Cifaldi Brothers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u0/tg/ualb3r8e5697d324e3dc2053862087_600x591.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This book is filled with dirty, sexual jokes meant for adults.

## 3. K is for Knifeball: an Alphabet of Terrible Advice by Avery Monsen and Jory John

![K is for Knifeball: an Alphabet of Terrible Advice by Avery Monsen and Jory John](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pw/k4/mzajq04k5697d32271dba376951030_500x500.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

If you're teaching your kids the alphabet, you'll appreciate this spoof.

## 4. You Have to Fking Eat by Adam Mansbach

![You Have to Fking Eat by Adam Mansbach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dz/i4/dsu5eg045697d325ab511645529661_600x470.jpg)

## 5. Pat the Zombie: a Cruel (Adult) Spoof by Aaron Ximm

![Pat the Zombie: a Cruel (Adult) Spoof by Aaron Ximm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qk/kq/msi07n0b5697d32320337099264901_418x500.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Are You My Boyfriend? by C. B. Bryza

![Are You My Boyfriend? by C. B. Bryza](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oc/qp/xjtfrpky5697d3256e682868169479_600x812.jpg)
