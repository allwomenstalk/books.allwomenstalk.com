# 7 Thought Provoking Quotes from Gone Girl about Life ...

If you haven't seen the movie or read the book, these quotes from Gone Girl won't spoil the plot for you. However, they will get you thinking about the way you live your life. The author, Gillian Flynn, includes dozens of thought provoking topics that will get your mind running. Once you're through reading the following interesting quotes from Gone Girl, make sure you pick up a copy of the novel. You won't regret a minute of the time you spend flipping through the pages.

## 1. Amended Memories

![Amended Memories](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ci/pc/t3y7o7e0_600x380.jpg)

Have you ever thought about your first kiss with someone, but it made you feel uncomfortable instead of joyful? When that happens, it usually means that the relationship is over. Maybe it has already been over for a long time. Either way, it's fascinating how something that once caused you so much happiness can turn into an awful memory. This is one of the quotes from Gone Girl that will make you wonder why your brain works the way that it does.

## 2. Reality's Ruse

![Reality's Ruse](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ov/ij/kco43osb_600x410.jpg)

Think about your personality for a second. Are you a flirty person, because that's what you feel most comfortable being, or because society has taught you to behave in that way? It's hard to tell whether you're actually being yourself or if you're just trying to be like the people around you and the ones you watch on TV. Everything we see has the potential to influence us.

## 3. A Soul's Significance

![A Soul's Significance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jr/vp/ybzc5i1o_600x341.jpg)

When you meet someone you're attracted to, you'll act differently than you usually act. The changes might be slight, but you'll stand up straighter, smile more, and behave like a lady. If you get into the habit of putting on a different persona, then is the person you consider your soul mate really your soul mate?

## 4. Scorned Survivors

![Scorned Survivors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vp/w5/tamig256_400x400.jpg)

If someone treats you poorly, you shouldn't put up with it, but you shouldn't spend your life moping over it either. People who have been scorned know more about life than people who have never been hurt. Use your knowledge to your advantage, and make something of yourself.

## 5. Public Perception

![Public Perception](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kl/eq/xvgqcz3x_400x400.jpg)

Would you want your grandmother knowing that you cheated on your boyfriend with some stranger you met at the bar? If not, you should probably rethink your decision to go home with him. Don't do anything that you'd be ashamed about people finding out, because people usually do find out, especially with the social media that we've all come to rely on.

## 6. Caring Kisses

![Caring Kisses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wo/cw/yync7847_600x338.jpg)

A kiss will feel different, depending on who gives it to you. If a stranger plants one on you, it's not a very special moment. When the love of your life kisses you, it's an entirely different experience. It'll make you feel loved, attractive, and wanted. It's one of the best feelings in the world.

## 7. Love's Influence

![Love's Influence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ci/e0/o0ai9drg_600x356.jpg)

People claim that your mate should bring out the best in you. However, that might not be true. Maybe your mate should just make you feel relaxed enough to act like yourself. Who knows? It's something to think about.

If you haven't read the book or seen the movie, make sure to do so immediately. You've never seen a story like this before, and you won't be able to stop thinking about it once you're finished with it. Are you a fan of Gillian Flynn yet?
