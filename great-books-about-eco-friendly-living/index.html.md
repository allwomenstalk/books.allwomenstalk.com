# 7 Great Books about Eco\-Friendly Living ...

If you are trying to incorporate environmentally friendly practices into your daily life, books about eco-friendly living can be very helpful. They will give you advice and tips on how to be green, and make the transition to green living much easier. Fortunately, there are plenty of wonderful books about eco-friendly living to choose from. The following books are a few of my top choices for learning more about eco-friendly living, and they will provide you with plenty of suggestions, some of which will even save you money!

## 1. The Eco Chick Guide to Life

![The Eco Chick Guide to Life](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v2/xc/t7zg6has_453x648.jpg)

"The Eco Chick Guide to Life: How to be Fabulously Green" is one of the books about eco-friendly living that will make transitioning to a green lifestyle easier. Starre Vartan wrote this wonderful book, and she has included many resources in her book that are very helpful. The chapter that focuses on eco-friendly fashion is especially great. It really helps show that you can be green and still look fashionable!

## 2. Gorgeously Green

![Gorgeously Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/le/as/jaqe2ush_400x500.jpg)

Written by Sophie Uliano, "Gorgeously Green" breaks down eco-living into 8 simple steps. The first chapter is devoted to relating to the readers what eco-living is all about, and the following seven chapters cover how to live in an environmentally friendly way. She covers such topics as environmentally friendly beauty products, fashion, cleaning supplies, and much more.

## 3. Green Book

![Green Book](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vs/ph/iiyay0gg_600x937.jpg)

A New York Times bestseller, "The Green Book: The Everyday Guide to Saving the Planet One Simple Step at a Time" features advice from Hollywood stars about green living. Justin Timberlake, Jennifer Aniston, and Ellen DeGeneres are just a few of the stars who offer simple and easy tips for eco-friendly living.

## 4. Plastic-Free

![Plastic-Free](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ux/wy/md5d9ufz_550x638.jpg)

Beth Terry's "Plastic-Free: How I Kicked the Plastic Habit and How You Can Too" is a great resource for those who want to use less plastic. Beth Terry is the author of this wonderful book, which she was inspired to write after reading an article about the plastic in the ocean. Terry provides many tips for going plastic free and also explains the importance of a plastic free life. You will definitely be inspired to use less plastic after reading this book!

## 5. Easy Green Living

![Easy Green Living](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f2/fo/m5hewp2e_409x500.jpg)

Reneé Loux's "Easy Green Living: The Ultimate Guide to Simple, Eco-friendly Choices for You and Your Home" is a wonderful book about eliminating toxic beauty and cleaning products from your life. This book goes over common toxic ingredients and provides safer alternatives for you and the environment. It also has recipes for cleaning products you can make yourself and helpful 5 step lists to make green living simple.

## 6. No More Dirty Looks

![No More Dirty Looks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tz/wt/ku4f0xtd_482x720.jpg)

Written by Siobhan O’Connor and Alexandra Spunt, “No More Dirty Looks: The Truth about Your Beauty Products – and the Ultimate Guide to Safe and Clean Cosmetics” will help you make informed choices about your beauty products. The authors enlisted the help of scientists, dermatologists, and make-up artists to provide you with safe eco-friendly makeup choices. They also give you the scoop on what isn’t safe and why.

## 7. No Impact Man

![No Impact Man](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gf/h3/a16hob6y_519x800.jpg)

Colin Beavan’s "No Impact Man" is a fantastic book about Beavan’s attempt to live without impacting the environment for one year. The book explains what it took for him and his family to do this and documents their experiences living 100 percent eco-friendly. This book is wonderful to read, and will give you plenty of ideas for incorporating eco-friendly living in your own life.

Living in an environmentally friendly way shouldn’t be hard. It should be easy, and you should feel good doing it. Thankfully, all of these books provide great tips for eco-friendly living. What books for eco-friendly living do you suggest?
