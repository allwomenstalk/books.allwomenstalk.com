# 6 Books about Personal Growth to Read  in 2018 for a Better You ...

Are you looking for some **books about personal growth**?The world of books can open up many different avenues and universes for us. If you are a fan of fiction, then you can escape to a complete world for a few chapters every evening, and if you are a non-fiction fan, then you can find mountains of inspiration and motivation in the infinite area of self-help and powerful memoirs. People go to the non-fiction and the self-help sections to help them discover and cultivate personal growth. This is something that is becoming more and more common, so if you fancy dipping your toe into the pool of personal growth, then here are six **books about personal growth** to help you get started.

## 1. The Miracle Morning by Hal Elrod

This is a book that emphasises all of the wonderful benefits of getting up early in the morning, much earlier than you might like but trust me when I say that it is a revelation! The author believes in taking the first hour of every day to set yourself up for success, and the book teaches you lots of techniques for doing just that. This is one of my favorite **books about personal growth**.

## 2. The Compound Effect by Darren Hardy

This book focuses on the key principal that your destiny is shaped by all of the decisions you make, both small and big. Every single day, minuscule decisions can have the effect of taking your life down a different path. Darren Hardy helps you to understand that you have the power in these decisive moments, and you can mold your own future in the way that you wish.

## 3. Big Magic by Elizabeth Gilbert

The author of the world famous "Eat, Pray, Love" is at it again with a book that offers lots of insights into the mysterious nature of inspiration, and all of the different circumstances in which we can find it. She talks about tackling things that we love as well as what we fear, and discusses changes of habits that you should make in order to live a more creative life.

## 4. The Success Principles by Jack Canfield

Jack Canfield is the creator of the famous "Chicken Soup for the Soul" series, and in this book, he teaches you how to increase your daily confidence and tackle tricky challenges. He wants you to live with passion and enthusiasm, and he does this by highlighting 64 timeless principles.

## 5. You Are a Badass by Jen Sincero

This has been one of the most popular personal growth books in recent times, a hilarious and raw work for people who “want to improve their lives but don’t want to get busted doing it”. It is truly hilarious, but you will also be touched by unexpectedly poignant moments along the way.

## 6. How to Stop Worrying and Start Living by Dale Carnegie

Anxiety is a massive issue in today’s fast-paced society, and personal growth guru Dale Carnegie offers lots of brilliant practical formulas that you can put into place in your own life to reduce your own feelings of anxiety, both in the workplace and in your personal life.
