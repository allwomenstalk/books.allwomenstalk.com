# The Bestselling Books from 2015 Every Woman Needs to Read ...

You can never read too many books. That's why it's always a great idea to check the Best Seller's list to see what others recommend. Of course, you don't have to do that, because the bestselling books from 2015 are right here:

## 1. Go Set a Watchman by Harper Lee

![Go Set a Watchman by Harper Lee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gk/kh/uttr9jm9565f5aa60f359736125680_331x500.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is written by the same woman who wrote To Kill A Mockingbird, which is why everyone raced to read it.

## 2. The Girl on the Train by Paula Hawkins

![The Girl on the Train by Paula Hawkins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ng/ap/o93irh5z565f5aa6d0192534432611_334x500.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

If you love books that surprise you all the way through, you'll enjoy this story. It's about an unreliable alcoholic who always watches a couple during her train ride.

## 3. All the Light We Cannot See Hardcover by Anthony Doer

![All the Light We Cannot See Hardcover by Anthony Doer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/di/oa/vxbec0br565f5aa90746e203169470_600x902.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is about a blind French woman and a German boy during World War II.

## 4. Grey: Fifty Shades of Grey as Told by Christian by E.L. James

![Grey: Fifty Shades of Grey as Told by Christian by E.L. James](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/az/vd/dapnd1xb565f5aa71721d762989688_325x500.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

You've already heard Anastasia's side of the story, so it's time to hear Christian's.

## 5. The MARTIAN by Andy Weir

![The MARTIAN by Andy Weir](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uh/hc/f3ttf8xq565f5aad0f708140114194_600x926.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is about a man who has walked on Mars, but may end up dying there.

## 6. SEE ME by Nicholas Sparks

![SEE ME by Nicholas Sparks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gb/mr/ywi1687k565f5ab0e44e2920364833_600x868.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

Like most of Sparks' novels, this is about a couple who faces intense problems in their passionate relationship.

## 7. The GUILTY by David Baldacci

![The GUILTY by David Baldacci](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fi/hx/s8uxalc0565f5ab06c3d6579519955_600x902.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is about a man who used to be a lethal assassin, but can no longer get the job done.

## 8. The Nightingale by Kristin Hannah

![The Nightingale by Kristin Hannah](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/os/njrnh2qh565f5aafb1e57151753969_600x912.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is about a brave woman during the time when the Nazis were invading France.

## 9. American Sniper by Chris Kyle

![American Sniper by Chris Kyle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ge/r7/rvf9p8lq565f5ab12316d926083286_600x906.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Even if you've seen the film, you should read this book about a Navy Seal in the US military.

## 10. Paper Towns by John Green

![Paper Towns by John Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tn/m3/k69tatkp565f5ab188fb8298596934_600x899.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Here's another book that was turned into a movie recently. It's about young love.

## 11. Gone Girl by Gillian Flynn

_ amazon.com_

This book is about love, lies, and lust. If you're going to read anything, make it this novel.

## 12. Big Little Lies

![Big Little Lies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bi/tc/o80z9sk6565f5ab717ba7225780584_600x900.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a thrilling novel about a murder in a small town.

## 13. Make Me: a Jack Reacher Novel by Lee Child

![Make Me: a Jack Reacher Novel by Lee Child](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u1/tk/dh5nctnu565f5ab91c77f443095466_600x930.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Stephen King is a fan of this series, so you know it's going to be thrilling.

## 14. The Stranger by Harlan Coben

![The Stranger by Harlan Coben](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ml/f4/kh70kik3565f5abb2d5f5166474295_600x908.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a suspense novel that'll make you question the people closest to you.

## 15. Me before You by Jojo Moyes

![Me before You by Jojo Moyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ok/si/pd6cdey4565f5aba99770456330138_600x920.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a heartbreaking novel about two people who couldn't be more different.

## 16. Ready Player One by Ernest Cline

![Ready Player One by Ernest Cline](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/rd/c3syqt2l565f5abba8fac294750318_600x925.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

If you love electronics, you'll love this book about the year 2044, when everyone lives more in the digital world than the real world.

## 17. The Good Neighbor by a.J Banner

![The Good Neighbor by a.J Banner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/n4/ghwijsrh565f5abd49b76754655656_600x900.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a suspense novel about a woman whose life ends up unravelling all at once.

## 18. Everything I Never Told You by Celeste Ng

![Everything I Never Told You by Celeste Ng](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fx/vr/uxm8xnps565f5abdf0026000347954_600x918.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This book takes place in the 70s. It's about a Chinese American family whose daughter is found in the local lake.

## 19. The Boys in the Boat by Daniel James Brown

![The Boys in the Boat by Daniel James Brown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ej/e9/kc3mb1do565f5abf87ba7428750465_600x920.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is about nine working-class boys who ended up at the Olympics in 1936.

## 20. The Liar by Nora Roberts

![The Liar by Nora Roberts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s0/n6/q0sssc5l565f5ac1c01af653433265_600x906.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

What would you do if you found out the man you loved was nothing but a lie?

## 21. Outlander by Diana Gabaldon

![Outlander by Diana Gabaldon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cg/rw/rjd9oblv565f5ac29acf2993625910_600x986.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This book was so good that it got turned into a television show!

## 22. The Silent Girls by Eric Rickstad

![The Silent Girls by Eric Rickstad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/xh/ris9h8kt565f5b86dad6a462914194_332x499.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a mystery novel about a man who turned in his detective's badge, but crime isn't done with him yet.

## 23. The Husband's Secret by Liane Moriarty

![The Husband's Secret by Liane Moriarty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jh/cj/p52s37r9565f5ac34a083107549724_600x900.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

A man wrote his wife a letter that was meant to be read after he died, but she finds it while he's still alive and well. It contains his deepest, darkest secret.

## 24. The CROSSING by Michael Connelly

![The CROSSING by Michael Connelly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/mx/zq75auia565f5ac6ab746096274326_600x931.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is about a retired detective who tries to help his step-brother, the lawyer, with a client.

## 25. ALL DRESSED in WHITE by Mary Higgins Clark and Alafair Burke

![ALL DRESSED in WHITE by Mary Higgins Clark and Alafair Burke](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s8/r1/njycul8e565f5ac62e4ec029534285_600x906.jpg)

[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is about a television producer that investigates the case of a missing bride.

What's the best book you've read in 2015? I highly suggest reading Gone Girl, Ready Player One, The Girl On the Train, and The Good Neighbor!
