# Enticing Books to Read if You Miss the Harry Potter Series ...

Harry Potter is one of those series that generations of readers will love and cherish all their lives. Just because J.K. Rowling ended the series doesn't mean you can't still read your way to another, more magical world! Lucky for you, Buzzfeed.com created this list of books to read if you miss Harry Potter!

## 1. [Six of Crows](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com) by Leigh Bardugo

_ [amazon.co.uk](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

**Why?** It's like Oceans Eleven meets Order of the Phoenix.

Gripping, twisty and a whole new world of magic – Six of Crows is your new obsession. It follows a rag tag bunch of teenage thieves and wizards, about to undertake the heist of their life. Honestly, just read it now. The best bit? The sequel – Crooked Kingdom_ is already out, and there are also three novels that are set in the same universe to get through, if you want to dive into the folklore.

Get it on Amazon, [£5.59](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 2. _The Call_ by Peadar O'Guilin

_ amazon.co.uk_

**Why?** It's like The Hunger Games meets Goblet of Fire

This unusual thriller focuses on Irish folklore and fairytale, turning the sidhe (or fairy folk) into a harsh and creative nemesis, that teenagers must battle against when they get called to the other side without warning. It's gory in parts, but it's a page turner – plus it's set in a boarding school.

Get it on _Amazon, £7.74_

## 3. _The Chrestomanci Series_ by Diana Wynne Jones

_ amazon.co.uk_

**Why?** It's like Philosopher's Stone meets Ella Enchanted

You may know Dianna Wynne Jones better as the author of Howl's Moving Castle, but the Chrestomanci series is my personal favourite. Set over seven books, it tells the story of Cat Chant who at first appears to have no magical ability whatsoever...

Get it on _Amazon, £2.80_

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 4. _Knights of the Borrowed Dark_ by Dave Rudden

_ amazon.co.uk_

**Why?** It's like Alien meets Chamber of Secrets

If you're craving some straight-up H.Po, then this is the book for you. Follow plucky young orphan Denizen Hardwick as he discovers powers he didn't know he had, and evil he didn't know existed. There's also lots of excellent female characters. The sequel is already out.

Get it on _Amazon, £6.99_

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 5. _Fangirl_ by Rainbow Rowell

_ amazon.co.uk_

**Why?** It's like Half-Blood Prince meets You've Got Mail

Centred around Cath, who writes fan fiction about a famous wizard and his friends, this book is sweet and sad and a weekend read. There are whole sections of her wizard fan fiction throughout the book – and her stories are so good that they released her fan fiction as its own full, separate novel, called _Carry On_.

Get it on _Amazon, £4_

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 6. _Rebel of the Sands_ by Alwyn Hamilton

_ amazon.co.uk_

**Why?** It's like Prisoner of Azkaban meets Aladdin

This book features a different kind of magic, and the most kick-ass heroine you'll meet in a while. Set in the desert of a steampunky Middle East, our girl Amani must flee through the sand, while discovering the powers that surround and envelop her.

Get it on _Amazon, £5.59_

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 7. _The Girl of Ink and Stars_ by Kiran Millwood Hargrave

_ amazon.co.uk_

**Why?** It's Goblet of Fire meets Labyrinth

There's a more subtle kind of magic in this book, as Isabella embarks on a quest to find her lost friend. There's old magic, living myths and secrets to solve. But she won't let that get in her way.

Get it on _Amazon, £5.24_

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. _Percy Jackson and the Lightning Thief_ by Rick Riordan

_ amazon.co.uk_

**Why?** It's Philosopher's Stone meets Hercules

Percy Jackson is just trying to live his life as a regular half-god, but ends up on a quest to find a stolen lightning bold and return it to Zeus. This is a proper romp, and there are plenty of books in the series to get through.

Get it on _Amazon, £3.99_

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 9. [The Rest of Us Just Live Here](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com) by Patrick Ness

![text, font, poster, product, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xb/dx/l8lc01a4594907c1ecf56529144080_500x500.jpg)

[amazon.co.uk](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

**Why?** It's Fantastic Beasts meets Buffy

Imagine Harry Potter from Dudley's perspective (or, more accurately, Arabella Figg's). In this book, Mikey's best friend is the ~ chosen one ~, and Mikey just has to get on with it. But why should "ordinary" mean "boring"?

Get it on [Amazon, £5.19](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 10. [Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com) by Ransom Riggs

![text, poster, advertising, brand, album cover,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d6/b5/cr336b1e594907c219099092662155_500x500.jpg)

[amazon.co.uk](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

**Why?** It's Half-Blood Prince meets X-Men

If you liked the flashbacks in Half-Blood Prince then this is the series for you. Focusing on a surreal orphanage during WW2, this novel swiftly turns into a mystery. There's some neat formatting too, with photographs scattered throughout the book. If you buy it on audiobook, you can download the photos separately.

Get it on [Amazon, £3.85](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. [The Secret of Platform 13](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com) by Eva Ibbotson

![text, poster, album cover, advertising, comics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a8/s9/smymsal0594907c2538fd404318788_500x500.jpg)

[amazon.co.uk](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

**Why?** It's like Philosopher's Stone meets The Princess Bride

This remains one of my all-time favourite books, and I am so excited for you to read it. A crew of magic creatures come through a magic portal at King's Cross station to retrieve the prince that was stolen from their kingdom as a child. But he doesn't want to come back...

Get it on [Amazon, £5.99](https://www.amazon.co.uk?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Listen to the audiobook [here](https://go.redirectingat.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
