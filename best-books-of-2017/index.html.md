# 20 Best Books from 2017 You Won't Be Able to Put down ...

What are the **best books of 2017**? Winter is the perfect time to snuggle up with a good book, and as the year is almost over, there are some great books that came out in the last 12 months that you might have missed. Here are the **best books of 2017** to get you started on a reading adventure you won't soon forget.

## 1. Ginny Moon: a Novel

![text, font, area, brand, book,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/im/xj11f5r45a40a676eb8de233664180_500x499.jpg)

$18.35 at [amzn.to](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Benjamin Ludwig's whip-smart, unforgettable novel is an illuminating look at one girl's journey to find her way home. It's definitely one of the **best books of 2017**. Available on Kindle, in hardcover, in paperback, as an audiobook, and on MP3 CD.

## 2. The Impossible Fortress: a Novel

![text, font, product, poster, area,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dx/hm/po1ldrp95a40a677411e3437272068_500x499.jpg)

$12.95 at [amzn.to](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A love letter to the 1980s, to the dawn of the computer age, and to adolescence-a time when anything feels possible-The Impossible Fortress will make you laugh, make you cry, and make you remember in exquisite detail what it feels like to love something-or someone-for the very first time. Available on Kindle, in hardcover, in paperback, as an audiobook, and on MP3 CD.

## 3. Eggshells

![green, yellow, text, font, graphic design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ty/w4/jush828e5a40a6774b29c104441194_500x499.jpg)

$11.55 at [amzn.to](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Debut author Caitriona Lally offers readers an exhilaratingly fresh take on the Irish love for lyricism, humor, and inventive wordplay in a book that is deeply charming, and deeply moving. Available on Kindle, in paperback, as an audiobook, and on MP2 CD.

## 4. One of the Boys: a Novel

![text, orange, font, line, area,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t4/td/gh8k4q655a40a67792f09664934633_500x499.jpg)

$12.10 at [amzn.to](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Little Fires Everywhere

![text, font, brand, advertising,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/td/sy/ebpgqsuo5a40a677b4acb558806800_500x499.jpg)

$15.28 at [amzn.to](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Lincoln in the Bardo: a Novel

![text, orange, font, orange, produce,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j8/k6/z5fsf7g95a40a67a4e1a2441641595_500x499.jpg)

## 8. Standard Deviation: a Novel

![text, product, font, product design, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fz/mi/gagl7joo5a40a67a2a346620374539_500x499.jpg)

## 9. The Weight of Ink

![text, font, calligraphy, flower, produce,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/jq/podfx9my5a40a67a4b1dc737989916_500x499.jpg)

## 10. Spoonbenders

![text, font, product, brand, label,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fn/v5/vrnomsq05a40a67aafcff464254035_500x499.jpg)

## 11. The Heart’s Invisible Furies: a Novel

## 12. 4 3 2 1: a Novel

## 17. Exit West: a Novel

![blue, text, purple, font, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d4/cs/gq64cc635a40a67e1f1d8701735140_500x499.jpg)

## 18. Beartown: a Novel

![text, water, aqua, font, water resources,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bo/l3/qrvwcmjw5a40a67e45362670233064_500x499.jpg)
