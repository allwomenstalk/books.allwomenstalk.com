# 5 Romance Authors You Should Read  ...

There aer so many great **romance authors you should read**. Since we can’t always get a good romance in real life, we look for it in books, movies or TV series. Reading a good book is always a nice pastime. And when it is combined with a fairy tale-like romance it’s even better. These romance authors have created characters that certainly catch the attention and worth a minute of your time. So add these **romance authors you should read** to your list.

## 1. Alice Clayton

New York Times bestselling author Alice Clayton is no stranger to romance. With three book series in her list of accomplishments (The Redhead Series, The Cocktail Series and the Hudson Valley Series) she knows how to create amazing characters and put them on their own amazing journey. Her books are filled with humor, breathtakingly beautiful characters and plenty of romance to make your heart race. That's why she tops my list of **romance authors you should read**.

## 2. Katy Evans

Another New York Times and USA Today bestselling author, Evans started her career as a self-published author. Her first book, "Real," became a best seller on numerous self-publishing marketplaces like Amazon. Katy Evans books include 4 series of books so far (Manwhore Series, Real Series, White House Series and Manhattan Series). Her characters are real, sexy, smart and above all know what they want in life and how to fight for it.

## 3. A. Zavarelli

A. Zavarelli is a USA Today and Amazon bestselling author. She is known to put her characters through difficult situations and seems to like to write about bad boys that end up heads over heels in love with the female characters she creates. Her books include the Boston Underworld Series, the Bleeding Hearts Series, and a number of standalones.

## 4. Cherise Sinclair

For those who desperately look for the next BDSM author to catch their attention, look no further. Cherise Sinclair is a New York Times and USA today bestselling author and is known to write stories that include humor, passion, great dominants and hot as hell sex scenes. Her Club Shadowlands Series counts 12 books to date and another one is coming up soon. In case you like to mix up your romance with some fantasy, Sinclair has you covered on that too. The Wild Hunt Legacy Series is for you. Equally interesting characters with the special ability to shift forms.

## 5. Jodi Ellen Malpas

New York Time bestselling author, Jodi Ellen Malpas, wrote her debut series in secret. She then decided to go into self-publishing. Her series (This Man Series) has been voted as one of the top romance novels of all time. She knows how to write intriguing characters and put them through situations that can either be extremely hot, extremely dramatic, extremely heartwarming, or all three. She counts another series (One Night series) and a number of stand alone novels to date.

If you are looking for a heart warming, sexy and fun book to spend your free time, make sure to give a try to these five authors. Their beautiful romance stories with interesting characters will definitely be a good pick for you.
