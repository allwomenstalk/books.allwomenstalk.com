# The Books on the Longlist for the Bailey's Prize for Women's Fiction 2016 ...

The Bailey’s Prize for Women’s Fiction is one of the UK’s premier literary awards. (Yes – that Bailey’s – cheers!). It is open to any female author who writes in the English language and the list usually has a good global representation and mix of ethnicities. Some are names familiar to you but over half this year’s longlist are debut novelists – definitely something to be proud of. The list of 20 will become 6 finalists with the prize winner being announced on June 7 in London.

## 1. A God in Ruins by Kate Atkinson

![A God in Ruins by Kate Atkinson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xc/nf/npw4gst856ebcb5a444cb823456246_500x500.jpg)

A touching story about an old fashioned man who faces daily challenges in a rapidly modernizing world.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Rush Oh! by Shirley Barrett

![Rush Oh! by Shirley Barrett](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ku/ws/y62ifgcl56ebcb5a919e9871360925_500x500.jpg)

Set in 1908, this book tells the charming story of a young girl’s coming of age during a tough whaling season in New South Wales.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Ruby by Cynthia Bond

![Ruby by Cynthia Bond](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/nc/dpj73u8r56ebcb6016d12192080426_600x925.jpg)

A truly memorable novel about a man and his campaign to protect the woman he loves from a town who wants to destroy her.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. The Secret Chord by Geraldine Brooks

![The Secret Chord by Geraldine Brooks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vy/fq/t7myiakf56ebcb5a4f62e451842938_500x500.jpg)

An ambitious novel that takes on the iconic biblical story of David and tries to tell it in a new and revolutionary way.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. The Long Way to a Small, Angry Planet by Becky Chambers

![The Long Way to a Small, Angry Planet by Becky Chambers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zh/ls/cg4z2nqm56ebcb5cd6607338727778_500x500.jpg)

Get wrapped up in this endlessly entertaining sci-fi romp that focuses on a young explorer adventuring across the galaxy.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. A Dictionary of Mutual Understanding by Jackie Copleton

![A Dictionary of Mutual Understanding by Jackie Copleton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k2/a5/ja5k1e3i56ebcb5bcfa44004047766_500x500.jpg)

If you loved Memoirs Of A Geisha, you will definitely be a fan of this new novel about family, love, forgiveness and pain.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Whispers through a Megaphone by Rachel Elliott

![Whispers through a Megaphone by Rachel Elliott](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mu/aa/o1oz4afr56ebcb5f986e3737446820_600x926.jpg)

A touching romantic novel about a shy woman who finally decides to venture out of her comfort zone in an attempt to find love.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. The Green Road by Anne Enright

![The Green Road by Anne Enright](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i5/kw/fmtjxjd956ebcb5d8a6b6328241139_500x500.jpg)

A classic feeling tale that spans the ups and downs of a family over thirty eventful years.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. The Book of Memory by Petina Gappah

![The Book of Memory by Petina Gappah](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/nr/q360tcw756ebcb5fdd780002028501_600x915.jpg)

An incredible book told from the perspective of an albino woman who is serving time in a Zimbabwe prison for a murder conviction.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 10. Gorsky by Vesna Goldsworthy

![Gorsky by Vesna Goldsworthy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gr/kq/jm8b2euf56ebcb615d546246380212_600x831.jpg)

A real page turner of a novel that incorporates elegance, romance and an addictive dose of danger.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. The Anatomist’s Dream by Clio Gray

![The Anatomist’s Dream by Clio Gray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xf/nn/v82113vn56ebcb5f7b0de061151858_500x500.jpg)

A brilliant novel that tells the story of a boy born with a skull abnormality who is forced to grow up in a travelling carnival.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 12. At Hawthorn Time by Melissa Harrison

![At Hawthorn Time by Melissa Harrison](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u4/bt/tgufamn356ebcb6007493580526810_500x500.jpg)

A sweeping novel that features the overlapping lives of a group of people, with themes including consumerism, identity, and people’s personal boundaries.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 13. Pleasantville by Attica Locke

![Pleasantville by Attica Locke](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mz/cn/mk0rfkpg56ebcb620276e450336712_500x500.jpg)

This novel by the producer of hit show Empire sees the return of lawyer Jay Porter who becomes mixed up in a tale of dangerous politics and intrigue.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 14. The Glorious Heresies by Lisa McInerney

![The Glorious Heresies by Lisa McInerney](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fk/w5/fdq5rtvs56ebcb61be2b4073507466_500x500.jpg)

A gripping and at times dark novel that highlights the culture and institutions of contemporary Ireland.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 15. The Portable Veblen by Elizabeth McKenzie

![The Portable Veblen by Elizabeth McKenzie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fw/q5/qs7iwv9d56ebcb62d40a0981897522_500x500.jpg)

A really unique novel about dysfunctional families, love, nature and money, and all the good and bad sides of those themes.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 16. Girl at Way by Sara Novic

![Girl at Way by Sara Novic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gg/hj/tgw0mpa156ebcb6602a34379934245_600x886.jpg)

An amazing story about a ten-year-old tomboy growing up in the war torn centre of Yugoslavia.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 17. The House at the Edge of World by Julia Rochester

![The House at the Edge of World by Julia Rochester](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ng/cg/jqeb4iag56ebcb6239e14671481465_500x500.jpg)

A brilliant debut novel that incorporates psychological drama and mystery to create a truly unforgettable read.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 18. The Improbability of Love by Hannah Rothschild

![The Improbability of Love by Hannah Rothschild](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lf/qs/jdffugfc56ebcb6412b09987202973_500x500.jpg)

Another debut novel, this time about the comings and goings in the background of a popular London auction house.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 19. My Name is Lucy Barton by Elizabeth Strout

![My Name is Lucy Barton by Elizabeth Strout](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uh/rv/pa1ubs9o56ebcb648f0d0622396206_500x500.jpg)

A touching story about a mother and daughter than revolves around a simple, unassuming visit to a hospital.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 20. A Little Life by Hanya Yanagihara

![A Little Life by Hanya Yanagihara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m0/hl/urtpe8ab56ebcb64c1ede592633169_500x500.jpg)

One of the most popular books of the last year, a story of four graduates classmates and their move to New York to find success and fortune.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

I hope you check out some of these great reads.

Congratulations to all the longlist authors and good luck!
