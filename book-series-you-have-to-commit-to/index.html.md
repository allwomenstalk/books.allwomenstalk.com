# Book Series You Have to Commit to ...

Being immersed in an engrossing book series is living in a fictitious world miles from your everyday without the withdrawal you get from reading a singular book. There is no need to suffer last page syndrome when there are many fabulous series to lose yourself in – book series you need commitment and passion for.

## 1. Discworld Series by Terry Pratchett

![Discworld Series by Terry Pratchett](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ok/cf/pc37cxpa5647336e628eb028167917_470x800.jpg)

An epic and at times hilarious fantasy adventure series that spans over a whopping forty-one books!
Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Outlander by Diana Gabaldon

![Outlander by Diana Gabaldon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w9/c2/ezlw34jm5647339888759365762203_470x700.jpg)

If you are a fan of the TV series, then you should definitely check out the full set of Outlander novels, giving you time travel and romance in abundance!

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. The Wheel of Time Series by Robert Jordan

![The Wheel of Time Series by Robert Jordan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ab/rs/ze3o0x0o564733c52773b116810334_600x920.jpg)

A wonderfully imaginative series of fourteen books that will satisfy any fan of Game of Thrones with its dragons, elves and other fantasy creatures.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Hercule Poirot Stories by Agatha Christie

![Hercule Poirot Stories by Agatha Christie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/iz/d2rbr4vf5647342769bb5402706187_600x929.jpg)

Poirot is one of Agatha Christie’s most beloved creations, and he appears in more than 30 novels that you can sink your teeth in to.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Nancy Drew Stories by Carolyn Keene

![Nancy Drew Stories by Carolyn Keene](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/di/f2/ojyocxhn564735351088d280247942_563x800.jpg)

If a female crime fighter is more your style, then you can’t go wrong with the extensive back catalogue of fun and intriguing Nancy Drew tales.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. A Dance to the Music of Time by Anthony Powell

![A Dance to the Music of Time by Anthony Powell](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zs/gr/zcfaud175647356118cf0883267850_600x916.jpg)

This is a grand and sweeping twelve-volume journey through the history and events of mid-twentieth century London. A really captivating series.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. The Vampire Huntress Legends by L.a. Banks

![The Vampire Huntress Legends by L.a. Banks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pa/z1/n3w54vjl564735ad9d4f3404664595_420x632.jpg)

From Philadelphia to Africa, this fourteen book vampire series will satisfy all of your paranormal fiction needs.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling

![Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cp/a5/asjysr8s564735e252d6f961573566_458x736.jpg)

If you are one of the few people who haven’t read Harry Potter, you need to fix that problem immediately; the books are so much better than the movies!

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. In Search of Lost Time by Marcel Proust

![In Search of Lost Time by Marcel Proust](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oa/i1/x8oyveiu564736181a641101117723_424x650.jpg)

For literature a little more on the ‘serious’ side, Marcel Proust provides over 3000 pages worth of in depth life experience of a man whose name you never learn.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 10. Dragonriders of Pern by Anne McCaffrey

![Dragonriders of Pern by Anne McCaffrey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nm/gf/eiah2ut95647364f9c213504542367_600x904.jpg)

Lose yourself in twenty volumes of this fantastical adventure featuring heroes and dragons at every turn!

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. Lone Wolf and Cub by Kazuo Koike and Goseki Kojima

![Lone Wolf and Cub by Kazuo Koike and Goseki Kojima](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/un/nftwna2i56473692622e3302697619_482x640.jpg)

Journey to ancient Japan in this epic manga classic, with twenty eight volumes of great adventure and stunning pictures to get through.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 12. The Dark Tower Series by Stephen King

![The Dark Tower Series by Stephen King](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fs/gw/mhvgbzzm564736bce993b000751089_540x720.jpg)

Part sci-fi, part Western, part fantasy and of course, part horror, this series by Stephen King will please fans of many different genres.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 13. Clarissa by Samuel Richardson

![Clarissa by Samuel Richardson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t0/z3/bsl021jg56473702aceb8887181759_600x968.jpg)

Not a long running series, but the classic story Clarissa is split in to three captivating volumes that add up to a staggering 100,000 pages!

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 14. The Dirk Pitt Adventures by Clive Cussler

![The Dirk Pitt Adventures by Clive Cussler](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g7/fp/i4uy92e25647319b2cd72862871367_600x967.jpg)

If you have a thirst for modern adventure with a swashbuckling hero, meet Dirk Pitt; a man of action with a nose for trouble and a reputation for solving problems. 23 books and counting.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 15. Montana Mavericks Series by Debra Salonen (and Others)

![Montana Mavericks Series by Debra Salonen (and Others)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vj/zo/w425faa35647319cb11d2583238885_600x885.jpg)

This series is a cheesy romance saga with different stories all set in Whitehorn, Montana. It’s not classic literature, but it’s easy reading and it’s kind of fun!

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 16. The Temeraire Books by Naomi Novik

![The Temeraire Books by Naomi Novik](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kj/cl/i7ttzr3w5647373345b3a327915291_600x904.jpg)

Go back in time to Napoleonic Europe – only this time there are dragons. This series explores a fascinating and very different version of our world.

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 17. The Baby-Sitters Club by Ann M. Martin

![The Baby-Sitters Club by Ann M. Martin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bm/iy/ees1hj1c5647376d9bff0008885959_600x870.jpg)

If you are in the mood for some serious nostalgia, then what better than to pick up a few great novel from the Baby-Sitters Club series that was so, so popular in the 80s and 90s.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 18. Poldark Series by Winston Graham

![Poldark Series by Winston Graham](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z1/gu/de3gatmq564737b5e5298586027744_531x750.jpg)

Turned into two different award winning TV series, Poldark is full of drama and romance and boasts twelve books to its name.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 19. James Bond Series by Ian Fleming (and Others)

![James Bond Series by Ian Fleming (and Others)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/db/hy/zf0xa5td564731a01a578517563021_600x900.jpg)

Get your literary fill of the most famous spy in the world, starting with the originals by Fleming and leading into the more modern interpretations by several different writers.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 20. Anita Blake Series by Laurell K. Hamilton

![Anita Blake Series by Laurell K. Hamilton](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eg/l9/febkpckp564737fbe8182900869823_402x648.jpg)

Another fast paced and engaging vampire hunter series for you to get addicted to, featuring loads of crazy twists and turns.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 21. Alex Cross Series by James Patterson

![Alex Cross Series by James Patterson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/hg/o8u1l4r356473839798e1128549238_600x922.jpg)

You can get lost in a total of twenty-one novels featuring James Patterson’s famous detective from Washington D.C.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
