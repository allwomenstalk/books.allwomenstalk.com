# 17 Books with Outstanding Endings to Absolutely Blow Your Mind ...

Sometimes a surprise ending turns an ok book into a winner. Sometimes a surprise ending can leave you feeling deflated and even a feeling you've been cheated by the author. How you feel about stories not turning out the way you imagine will have you ever reaching for or running away from these books with surprise endings.

## 1. The Murder of Roger Ackroyd by Agatha Christie

![The Murder of Roger Ackroyd by Agatha Christie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lw/cm/qgw807vl56d4c23dad296753192331_500x500.jpg)

An installment in the phenomenally popular Hercule Poirot series, many critics believe The Murder Of Roger Ackroyd to be the very best in the Poirot franchise, Agatha Christie has all of her best plot twist expertise on hand from start to finish!

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Water for Elephants by Sara Green

![Water for Elephants by Sara Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k0/ba/plp5kpnb56d4c23ee89f6774011001_600x900.jpg)

Water For Elephants isn’t necessarily a page turning plot twister full of unsolvable mysteries, but the book’s ending will definitely come as a surprise to everybody that reads it.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Fight Club by Chuck Palahniuk

![Fight Club by Chuck Palahniuk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/be/pz/kypdrg7o56d4c23e716e7099497239_500x500.jpg)

The cult of Fight Club is one that has permeated all of our pop culture sine the Brad Pitt and Ed Norton film adaptation, but it is still worth giving the amazing original book a read.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Gone Girl by Gillian Flynn

![Gone Girl by Gillian Flynn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/e2/e4oq9ld656d4c23e591c6170995502_500x500.jpg)

Gone Girl took over the world to become the absolute must read of a few years ago, and much of its hype came from its unpredictable and crazy plot twists and ending.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Sharp Objects by Gillian Flynn

![Sharp Objects by Gillian Flynn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k5/tn/wadli01m56d4c23e6383a409909084_500x500.jpg)

And if you enjoyed Gone Girl, you might want to look further back in Flynn’s career to her 2007 novel Sharp Objects, another fantastic novel full of surprises.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. The Raven Cycle Series by Maggie Stiefvater

![The Raven Cycle Series by Maggie Stiefvater](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/il/yb/mr5bjzmz56d4c23ec9b0c241530632_500x500.jpg)

This is a super addictive young adult series of books that will have you reaching straight for the next installment every time you finish each novel!

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. The Adoration of Jenna Fox by Mary E. Pearson

![The Adoration of Jenna Fox by Mary E. Pearson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q1/n1/cgmfnbng56d4c241bf34c884252245_500x500.jpg)

This is one of those stories that will literally make you questions everything! A teenage girl wakes up from a coma and needs to find out every single fact about her life from scratch.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Fingersmith by Sarah Waters

![Fingersmith by Sarah Waters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wx/eg/gg2lf3t356d4c243493be517066645_600x936.jpg)

Fingersmith is an enthralling tale set in Victorian London that involves a hidden lesbian love affair and so many plot twists that you will end up dizzy!

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 9. We Were Liars by E. Lockhart

![We Were Liars by E. Lockhart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k9/b0/y076zamh56d4c24396ae4647518208_600x894.jpg)

A gripping book about four teenagers known as ‘the liars’ who spend a summer on a private island where many secrets and surprises are revealed.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 10. Atonement by Ian McEwan

![Atonement by Ian McEwan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e9/nn/hqwoglsg56d4c2414a4aa570889078_500x500.jpg)

A modern classic set in the midst of the Second World War that centers around the devastation that a simple lie can wreak on a cast of character’s lives.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. Caught by Harlan Coben

![Caught by Harlan Coben](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/di/m3/ph1ddlnv56d4c242abbf8936122598_500x486.jpg)

A high octane and tense novel about an intrepid television reporter who works on bringing down sexual offenders.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 12. My Sister’s Keeper by Jodi Picoult

![My Sister’s Keeper by Jodi Picoult](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k0/l6/cma69goy56d4c242aa737403684350_500x500.jpg)

A quite amazing novel that tells the dramatic and emotional tale of two sisters; one with life threatening cancer and one whose apparent purpose is to serve as a readily available organ donor. Ends with a real bang.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 13. Big Little Lies by Liane Moriarty

![Big Little Lies by Liane Moriarty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sd/tq/dpqnbh3g56d4c2448e554008354723_500x500.jpg)

It seems from the onset like a typical novel about marriage, but Big Little Lies goes down paths that you would never have imagined!

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 14. Defending Jacob by William Landay

![Defending Jacob by William Landay](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/il/rw5c9xtz56d4c24494c07039671782_500x500.jpg)

A modern legal thriller that contains all of the best classic feeling genre elements while also bringing an innovative and at times shocking conclusion.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 15. The Girl on the Train by Paula Hawkins

![The Girl on the Train by Paula Hawkins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a2/xz/gnns1zr556d4c24642aed143853643_500x500.jpg)

Very much in the vein of Gone Girl, The Girl On The Train is a recent novel that took the reading world by storm and provided an irresistible turn paging experience all the way to the end.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 16. All Our Yesterdays by Cristin Terrill

![All Our Yesterdays by Cristin Terrill](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mn/p1/jk6ikpkb56d4c245dc71c330281509_500x500.jpg)

A gripping book in which the protagonist finds themselves trapped in a military base with only the voice of a mysterious boy in the next cell to seek information from.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 17. Before I Go to Sleep by S. J. Watson

![Before I Go to Sleep by S. J. Watson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mt/y8/z1xter8656d4c2461aad0289093132_500x500.jpg)

Another novel recently turned in to a movie, Before I Go To Sleep tells the mysterious tale of a woman who suffers with daily amnesia after a savage attack.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 18. The Maze Runner by James Dashner

![The Maze Runner by James Dashner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/rl/r2qg0roh56d4c248ab0e5795028874_600x923.jpg)

The first in a popular young adult series about a group of teenagers who are mysteriously trapped in a high walled compound surrounded by a huge and dangerous maze.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 19. The Westing Game by Ellen Raskin

![The Westing Game by Ellen Raskin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dn/q4/sxt74g8856d4c248d8d9c067950246_600x847.jpg)

A quirky and addictive mystery that involves a group of strangers being involved in the will of a recently deceased eccentric millionaire.

Buy at: [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Do you like plot twists or do you prefer endings to meet your own expectations?
