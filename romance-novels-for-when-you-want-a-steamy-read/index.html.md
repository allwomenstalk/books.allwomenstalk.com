# 15 Romance Novels for when You Want a Steamy Read ...

For some reason, there are lots of people out there who love reading but seem to regard steamy, saucy novels as some sort of lower art form. Sure, they might not be winning a Pulitzer Prize any time soon, but they definitely have their own place in the literary world! You can read them for cheap entertainment, you can read them to get inspiration, and you can certainly read them if you are looking to get in the mood! Here are fifteen **romance novels for when you want a steamy read**.

## 1. The Kiss Quotient by Helen Hoang

This is an addictive read about a successful businesswoman who hires an escort, but doesn’t anticipate the fact that she is going to fall head over heels for him! She also happens to have Asperger’s which adds an interesting and refreshing dynamic.
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 2. One True Loves by Taylor Jenkins Reid

A really absorbing story about a woman whose long lost husband reappears on the scene years after his disappearance, with the added complication that she has now moved on and found love with another person.
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 3. Intercepted by Alexa Martin

If you like sports AND romance, then you will love this novel about the girlfriend of an NFL player whose life gets more complicated when they break up and she starts dating one of his team mates!
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 4. Pas De Deux: a Dance for Two by Lynn Turner

This is a romance that is set in the world of ballet, a world that can be incredibly sexy and also incredibly stressful at the same time!
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 5. Scoring off the Field by Naima Simone

Another book set around the NFL, this time about a star player who and his assistant, who have been friends since childhood but under the surface, something much more passionate is burning.
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 6. Bully by Penelope Douglas

If young adult angst is something that gets you hot under the collar, then you will love this story of high school lust, first loves, and broken hearts!
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 7. Naked in Death by J.D. Robb

This is the first in a series of novels that combines some near future intrigue with a strong friendship that turns in to so much more along the way!
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 8. Cherish Me by Farrah Rochon

A really gripping love story about a couple who seem perfect from the outside but are going through their issues just like the rest of us. All set amidst the sexy backdrop of Rome!
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 9. When All the Girls Have Gone by Jayne Anne Krentz

This is a novel that is filled with unexpected twists as well as some great steamy scenes thanks to a super feisty heroine!
Buy here: [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 10. Wicked and the Wallflower by Sarah MacLean

If you want something that is historical but accessible and whole bunch of steamy fun at the same time, then look no further than Sarah MacLean’s books! This happens to be one of my favorites by her.
Buy here: [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. Dangerous Promise by Megan Hart

This is set in a futuristic world where a female bodyguard is tasked with protecting a guy who embodies the exact opposite of what she believes in. Of course, you know what is going to happen next!
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 12. Misfits by Garrett Leigh

This is a great one for foodies, set in the vibrant London food scene about a long-term relationship between two people who share a passion and a love for restaurants as their two main connections!
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 13. Haven by Rebekah Weatherspoon

A steamy, sweeping story set in the misty mountains where the isn’t much else to do than fall in total lust with a muscled, bearded heartthrob!
Buy here: [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 14. Fortitude Smashed by Taylor Brooke

A story that explores one of the many variations of opposites attract, this time in the form of a cop and an art thief!
Buy here: _amazon.com_

## 15. The Bittersweet Bride by Vanessa Riley

A gripping tale about a widow who is forced to remarry in order to protect her son’s inheritance, but her life is turned upside down when the small matter of her first love returning comes into play!
Buy here: [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
