# 17 Music Memoirs to Put on Your Reading List ...

If you've ever wondered what it's really like to be a rock star, now's your chance: there are so many memoirs out there! From the godmother of punk, Patti Smith, to the husband of Beyonce (and a musician in his own right) Jay-Z, every musician with his or her salt is telling all. Here are some excellent music memoirs to add to your must-read list.

## 1. Rat Girl by Kristin Hersh

![Rat Girl by Kristin Hersh](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ci/hv/otf6x83t562fa275a04b1819270021_600x914.jpg)

Price: $11.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Covering much more of her life than just her time in Throwing Muses, this book is really, really good.

## 2. Just Kids by Patti Smith

![Just Kids by Patti Smith](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hn/c8/qqedjjhj562fa27553f51033726023_333x500.jpg)

Price: $10.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is hands-down one of the best music memoirs I've ever read - she's obviously as talented a writer as she is a musician.

## 3. Cash by Johnny Cash

![Cash by Johnny Cash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qp/s7/n0ujym1m562fa27779a1f373247334_600x930.jpg)

Price: $9.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The take-away from this moving memoir? Cash was a great man, sure, but he never saw himself that way.

## 4. Coal Miner’s Daughter by Loretta Lynn

![Coal Miner’s Daughter by Loretta Lynn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n5/qn/rhfy4vqp562fa27585fe8216685223_323x500.jpg)

Price: $11.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Whether you're a fan of her music or not, this is a must-read.

## 5. Life by Keith Richards

![Life by Keith Richards](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m2/xm/uip4cbs1562fa277e25e1284185590_600x911.jpg)

Price: $9.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Yes, his life is as crazy as you'd imagine, and yes, he talks about most of it in this book.

## 6. Bound for Glory by Woody Guthrie

![Bound for Glory by Woody Guthrie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a8/j8/ig8n8suy562fa27b61db6019938709_600x911.jpg)

Price: $12.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Because I'm a fan, I'm biased, but this book is so unique, like the writer himself.

## 7. When I Left Home by Buddy Guy

![When I Left Home by Buddy Guy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p4/ce/quzzz6tw562fa27a1ea72495396948_319x500.jpg)

Price: $9.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A must-read for any fan, or anyone who just loves blues music.

## 8. Slash by Slash

![Slash by Slash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pe/l7/cuu5849o562fa27ac03aa772649638_331x500.jpg)

Price: $8.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I'm not even a Guns N Roses fan, but I liked this book anyway.

## 9. The Dirt by Motley Crue

![The Dirt by Motley Crue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ao/vj/w50hfu8g562fa27d594e1871682707_600x880.jpg)

Price: $13.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I'm DEFINITELY not a Crue fan, but this book is stellar for its depiction of the easy excesses Los Angeles in a particular era.

## 10. Chronicles by Bob Dylan

![Chronicles by Bob Dylan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/id/ib/h6dbuqv4562fa27e8b99f747151443_600x921.jpg)

Price: $11.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The thing I loved about this book is how it details the crazy ways Dylan found inspiration.

## 11. Scar Tissue by Anthony Kiedis

![Scar Tissue by Anthony Kiedis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/cn/h9xk80ho562fa2802840c918327589_600x910.jpg)

Price: $9.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Honest and harrowing, this book is equal parts music memoir and addiction tale.

## 12. Anger is an Energy by John Lydon

![Anger is an Energy by John Lydon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/an/nd/p1si82rt562fa2812e734370425420_600x906.jpg)

Price: $9.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Sort of the prequel to Rotten, the book and the man.

## 13. Girl in a Band by Kim Gordon

![Girl in a Band by Kim Gordon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ix/dk/ojgtkgu4562fa28394e97276605139_600x903.jpg)

Price: $12.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The short story: she never wanted to be in a band. The long story is much more interesting. Read this book, now!

## 14. M Train by Patti Smith

![M Train by Patti Smith](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pu/vy/va3uxx4h562fa28316232483474673_600x922.jpg)

Price: $12.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I know, I know: another Smith memoir? This one's different though, and contains stuff that happened as well as stuff Smith wishes and dreams happened. Fascinating!

## 15. Miles by Miles Davis

![Miles by Miles Davis](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a3/j7/n5y2cmtq562fa28463e74669693587_600x912.jpg)

Price: $13.59 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is a good read, though it's disheartening to learn what should have been obvious: that racism was real, even for Davis, as famous as he was.

## 16. Never Broken by Jewel

![Never Broken by Jewel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/un/kg/cbky6udl562fa2858f948290727799_600x905.jpg)

Price: $12.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You've probably heard brief sketches of Jewel's story, but her memoir goes into greater detail, making you adore her even more.

## 17. Words without Music by Philip Glass

![Words without Music by Philip Glass](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y8/sz/zghvx08x562fa2866f1d3749794995_600x881.jpg)

Price: $14.49 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
An amazing read, even if you aren't a music fan in any genre at all.
