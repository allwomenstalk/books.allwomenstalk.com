# 8 Bullet Journal Spreads to Inspire Your Own ASAP ⏱ ...

There are so many amazing **bullet journal spreads to inspire your own journal**.

I started keeping my bullet journal at the beginning of October, and it has changed my life. I love how it is so customizable, and how I don’t have to feel like I’m “off-theme.” My theme is me and my happiness!

If you don’t know what a bullet journal is, the official website defines it as: “a customizable and forgiving organization system. It can be your to-do list, sketchbook, notebook, and diary, but most likely, it will be all of the above. It will teach you to do more with less.” So, basically, a bullet journal is whatever the heck you want it to be!
